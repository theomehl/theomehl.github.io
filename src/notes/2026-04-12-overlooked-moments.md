I recently heard someone describe the fact that within everyday life, there are a lot of overlooked moments. A bird basking in the sun. The soft rustling of leaves. The presence of someone causing your heart to beat quickly with excitement. The way sunlight shining through a window casts patterns on the wall, shimmering and swaying gently. This idea fascinates me, and I feel inspired to make note of all the little overlooked moments in my own life.
