why prodigious?

Every now and then I go back and rewatch one of my favorite movies, October Sky. Quentin, one of the main characters, uses the word prodigious as a positive response to something where we might normally say 'awesome!' For some reason that always stuck with me and makes its way into my vocabulary.

If you haven't watched the movie before, you should at least once. It's an interesting look into a small coal town in West Virginia and a group of friends dreaming about things beyond it. Based on the memoir of NASA engineer Homer Hickam.
